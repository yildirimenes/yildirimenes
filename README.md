<h2>📌 About Me</h2>

✨ I am a Mobile Software Engineer with over 2 years of experience in crafting high-performance, scalable, and user-centric mobile ecosystems.

🚀 I possess an equal level of advanced expertise in both **Kotlin** and **Jetpack Compose** for native applications, as well as **React Native** for modern cross-platform solutions. This dual proficiency allows me to perfectly balance the power of native performance with the agility of rapid development cycles.

🛠️ **Tech Stack:** I specialize in modern tools like **Hilt (DI)**, **Retrofit**, **GraphQL**, and **Room**. Furthermore, I leverage **Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP)** to share business logic seamlessly and apply **Clean Architecture (MVVM)** principles to build maintainable, enterprise-grade projects.

📈 Committed to technical excellence, I manage the full app lifecycle end-to-end and share my development journey on my **YouTube channel (ComposeCrafting)**.
